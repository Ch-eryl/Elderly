        Help Contact

        Speech features are not supported on this device. You can still use large buttons, but voice input may not work. Try Chrome.

        Microphone Permission Needed

        Allow the microphone to speak and be heard. In Chrome, click the mic icon in the address bar and select Allow.

        About Us

        We help older adults do essential tasks without a screen. Speak naturally to book appointments, set medication reminders, manage shopping, and reach family help.

        How To Use

        • Tap the big mic button and allow microphone access.
        • Say a simple command like “Book appointment with Dr Smith on December 5 at 3 pm”.
        • Use large action buttons if voice isn’t available.
        • Save a help contact number, then say “Call help”.

        Benefits

        • No tiny text or complex menus—just clear voice.
        • Fast access to appointments, medications, shopping, and help.
        • Works with family support via a saved phone contact.
        • Privacy-friendly: data stored locally on your device.
